05 — Support / frequently asked

Answers, before
you have to ask.

How long until my build ships?

+

Curated tiers ship within 5 business days. Bespoke configurations require 7–14 days because of the full 72-hour burn-in protocol. A live tracker becomes available the moment your order is paid.

What if a part is incompatible?

+

It will not let you. The configurator runs a compatibility engine on every change — socket, TDP envelope, GPU length, AIO clearance, PSU headroom, RAM frequency support. You only see options that work.

Do you offer financing?

+

Up to 24 months interest-free with our partner Tinkoff. The estimate updates live below the total.

Warranty terms?

+

Three years on parts, lifetime on assembly. RMA is handled through a single ticket — we ship a replacement before you ship the old one back.

Can I bring my own parts?

+

Yes. Mark them as "BYO" during configuration. We perform a free inspection on arrival and either assemble with them or quote a fair trade-in.
